“You need **Claude Code** plus a real **agent system**: multiple roles, tools, and review — not one generic reply.”

**[Solution — 12–28s]**  
“We’re running a **7-day Seattle cohort**: **Claude Forge** — **eleven agents**, **MCP**, slash commands. Educational only.”

“Link in bio · `{{signup_url}}` · Not affiliated with Anthropic/YouTube/third-party Forge projects.”
