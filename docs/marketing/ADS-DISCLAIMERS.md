# Advertising and post disclaimers (education / tools)

Use **one** short disclaimer on paid social and near CTAs. Adjust for character limits.

## English — short (social)

`Educational cohort for founders & teams. Not affiliated with YouTube, Anthropic, Obsidian, or third-party agent projects (e.g. OpenClaw). No guaranteed business, token, or AI outcomes. Verify tool terms and data handling yourself.`

## English — medium (blog footer)

`This content is for educational purposes only. Tools (LLMs, Obsidian, agent software, etc.) change terms and pricing. Nothing here is legal, financial, or professional advice. Results depend on your use case; verify security and compliance before ingesting company or customer data.`

## Korean — short (네이버·티스토리)

`창업·팀 교육 목적입니다. 특정 수익·비용 절감을 보장하지 않습니다. 유튜브·Anthropic·옵시디언 및 제3자 에이전트 프로젝트와 무관합니다. 데이터·보안은 각사 정책을 확인하세요.`

## Paid ads (Meta / Google-style)

- Do **not** claim guaranteed **95% token savings**, **funding**, or **revenue** unless you have substantiation and platform approval.  
- Prefer: **professional development**, **workflow training**, **Obsidian + local agent**, **DIKW**, **Seattle-area cohort**.  
- Target **founders** and **startup employees** where platform policies allow; follow **financial services** and **AI** ad rules if applicable.

## Lead magnet / signup

Include: “By requesting materials, you confirm you understand this is educational content and not a consulting engagement.”
