# Advertising and post disclaimers (education / tools)

Use **one** short disclaimer on paid social and near CTAs. Adjust for character limits.

## English — short (social)

`Educational cohort for founders & teams. Not affiliated with YouTube, Anthropic, or third-party Claude Forge, MCP, or community projects. No guaranteed business, token, or AI outcomes. Verify Anthropic API terms and data handling yourself.`

## English — medium (blog footer)

`This content is for educational purposes only. Tools (Claude Code, Claude Forge, LLM APIs, MCP servers, etc.) change terms and pricing. Nothing here is legal, financial, or professional advice. Results depend on your use case; verify security and compliance before connecting repos, APIs, or customer data.`

## Korean — short (네이버·티스토리)

`창업·팀 교육 목적입니다. 특정 수익·비용 절감을 보장하지 않습니다. 유튜브·Anthropic 및 제3자 Forge/MCP 프로젝트와 무관합니다. API·데이터는 각사 정책을 확인하세요.`

## Paid ads (Meta / Google-style)

- Do **not** claim guaranteed **95% token savings**, **funding**, or **revenue** unless you have substantiation and platform approval.  
- Prefer: **professional development**, **workflow training**, **Claude Code + Forge**, **MCP**, **Seattle-area cohort**.  
- Target **founders** and **startup employees** where platform policies allow; follow **financial services** and **AI** ad rules if applicable.

## Lead magnet / signup

Include: “By requesting materials, you confirm you understand this is educational content and not a consulting engagement.”
